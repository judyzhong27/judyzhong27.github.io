Building a product line, one candy spoon at a time

Graphic design for merchandise and branding work created for student-run startup Candy Cutlery aiming to reduce plastic waste

OVERVIEW

Candy Cutlery are hard candy utensils that can be used to eat desserts and drinks, and then eaten as a second snack! Their products are 100% biodegradable, 100% plastic-free, dairy-free, and gluten-free.

The company is a startup from my hometown run by 3 students who wanted to reduce the environmental impact of single-use plastics. They produce 4 flavours of candy spoons and even candy shot glasses!

CLIENT

Main task

In September of 2018, their co-founders, Liyan and Lyn reached out to me to create some graphics for their brand new Candy Cutlery merchandise line to be released for the holiday season, now available on their Candy Cutlery Shopify store.

Their goal was to increase engagement with the Candy Cutlery brand, outside of their main products of candy spoons and candy glasses. It was another low-cost revenue stream for the company.

Candy Cutlery branding

The Candy Cutlery brand illustrates a fun, youthful, and memorable feeling while still maintaining a professional demeanor business owners and consumers can trust and respect.

Candy Cutlery’s visual identity comprises mainly of the colour red with variations of black and white. Created from a custom handmade type face, the new logo embodies a playful, lively and fun feeling that sets the tone for the brand.
