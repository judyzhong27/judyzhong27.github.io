Dec 2018

Illustration, graphic, logo design

Background

Candy Cutlery is a Canadian company that sells hard candy utensils that can be used to consume your drink or dessert. I partnered with them in December 2018 when their cofounder, Lyn Chen, approached me about doing some freelance design work for their upcoming line of Candy Cutlery merchandise.



I took on the project in December 2018. The project gave me lots of creative freedom - I designed illustrations and various logos to showcase Candy Cutlery's playful and environmentally-conscious branding nature.
