Creating content for a student group building tech for social good

Content creation that promotes technology as a solution for social good projects that resonates with students and non-profit organizations.

OVERVIEW

UW Blueprint is a student-run organization at the University of Waterloo. Their focus is on making technological solutions more accessible to non-profit organizations by allocating student resources, including designers, developers, product managers, and project leads to different projects each term.

As their content director for the Summer and Fall 2019 term, my priorities were to manage Blueprint's digital presence through running their Medium account, content for the Project Showcase, and social media for upcoming events and recruitment.
