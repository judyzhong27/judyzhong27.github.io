About Me

I'm a first year Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a strong interest in interaction design, product design, and web development. I am working on personal projects that look to optimize the user experience of technologies in our daily lives by conducting user research, prototyping with tools like Sketch and Figma, making awesome graphics with Adobe Illustrator and experimenting with various interfaces and interactions.

Outside of academics, I write for and edit the university newspaper the Iron Warrior, explore new restaurants, places and recipes on my blog, am a regular member of UW's Badminton Club and act as a mentor to high school students as an Engineering Ambassador.

Design & Me

My passion for UI/UX design stems from the desire to work on a well-functioning society through the seamless implementation of design solutions. My personal interest and my enrolment in one of the top engineering schools in Canada has given me a multidisplinary, integrative design and technology view on approaching community issues and how we can achieve harmony and efficiency in multiple industries.
