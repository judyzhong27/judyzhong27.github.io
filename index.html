Profile

I'm a first year Systems Design Engineering student at the University of Waterloo with a strong interest in interaction design, product design, and web development. I am working on personal projects that look to optimize the user experience of technologies in our daily lives through prototyping with Sketch and experimenting with interfaces and interactions.

Outside of academics, I write for the university newspaper, explore new restaurants, places and recipes on my blog, and act as a mentor to high school students as an Engineering Ambassador.

Design & Me

My passion for UI/UX design stems from the desire to work on a well-functioning society through the seamless implementation of design solutions. My personal interest and my enrolment in one of the top engineering schools in Canada has given me a multidisplinary, integrative design and technology view on approaching community issues and how we can achieve harmony and efficiency in multiple industries.
