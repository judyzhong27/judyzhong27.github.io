Design challenge

How might we showcase more curated content to increase content discoverability and retailer* presence within our app?
*Retailers refer to large to small businesses that pay reebee to have their flyer on our platforms weekly.
SOLUTION
We redesigned the previous reebee home screen to encompass a modern design and a new featured section. The new home screen promotes more discoverability for the user curated by our in-house team to bring our customers closer to better content.

The second part to our solution is a less-cluttered home screen on our mobile app will bring our app to modern design standards.
APPROACH
This project was initiated and pushed for by business goals and our stakeholders. We worked closely with the sales team to decide on the content showcased in the featured section and the best way to highlight our retailers in our app.

Final Solution: Home Screen Redesign

PART THREE:
EMPTY STATE SCREENS

The final part of this project was implementing empty-state screens to replace previously blank content in an empty shopping list, search results, and favourite stores list.

By adding actionable content and encouraging copy, we can emphasize our brand voice throughout the app and encourage users to use multiple app features.

Key performance indicators (KPIs)
